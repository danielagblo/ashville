Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about joining Ashville School.

We accept applications year-round, but most families apply in January for our main September intake. Early application is encouraged as seats in our Creche and Nursery programs fill up quickly.
While we focus on full-day immersive Montessori education, we do have flexible morning-only sessions for our youngest learners (Creche level) to help them transition into school life.
Our fee structure varies by grade level. Please download our latest Prospectus for a detailed breakdown of tuition, admission, and facility fees.
